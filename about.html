About

Who are you??

Hello! I am Movpasd. I am 23 years old. I studied physics at university and now I write software so I can eat.

Why are you here???

I made this personal blog using Pelican as an exercise to practice CSS and Jinja templating. The styles are 100% homemade, with no CSS pre-processor. I want to use this space as a scratchpad for thoughts and ideas, so that they are written down in words and not just floating around in my head, and so I can share them with friends. And who knows, maybe it will make me into an Internet micro-celebrity. One can dream.

What are you doing????

I like things which make you think differently about the world, or make you realise you've been taking a concept for granted. I expect I will be writing a lot about topics which I find interesting on here, which include:

  • maths
  • physics
  • software
  • sci-fi
  • worldbuilding (esp. conlangs)
  • those Lego videos on YouTube where they build robots to climb over walls
  • psychology & mental health

Why is it called that?

During the Second Punic War, the Romans besieged and then invaded Syracuse, Archimedes' home town. The story goes that a Roman soldier burst into his place with instructions to bring him to the Roman commander, but an unconcerned Archimedes, busy with a mathematical proof, told him "do not disturb my circles" -- his last words, because the soldier, enraged by his lack of deference, killed him!
